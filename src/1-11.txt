Inputs that would most likely uncover bugs in word counting program:

Whitespace only (tabs, newlines, spaces in any combination)
Empty input (zero characters)
Just EOF with no other input
Single word with no whitespace
Leading whitespace before words
Trailing whitespace after words
Leading and trailing whitespace
Multiple consecutive whitespace characters between words
Extremely long single word
Multiple extremely long words
Binary data (non-text input)
Input at integer max length
Input exceeding integer max length
